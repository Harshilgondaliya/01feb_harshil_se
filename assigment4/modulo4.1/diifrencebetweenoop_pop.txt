// What is Deference between OOP & POP ?


|| OOP ||

-> Programs are divided into parts known as objects.

-> The main focus of OOP is on the data.

-> It follows bottom-up approach.

-> It has access specifiers such as public,private etc.

-> It provides data hiding,data associated with the program.so security is provide.

-> Ease of modification.

-> Examples : java,perl




------------------------------------------


|| POP ||

-> In this programs are divided into function.

-> The main focus of POP is on the procedures.

-> It follows top-down approach.

-> In this most functions use global data.

-> POP dose not have any access specifiers.

-> POP dose not provide any data security.

-> Modification is difficult.

-> Examples : C,COBOL etc..
